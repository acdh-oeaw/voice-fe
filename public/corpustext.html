VOICE 3.0 Online - released 23 Sept 2021

New features to explore

Enhanced search functions

In VOICE 3.0 Online, the previous interfaces VOICE Online and VOICE POS Online have been merged and refined. The new interface includes entirely new query options that make it possible to search for select mark-up of spoken language (pauses, laughter, overlaps, etc.). Queries for words and lexical tokens, POS, lemmas and mark-up can be flexibly combined and modified with regular expressions and placeholders. For details see the VOICE 3.0 Online search manual.

Additional filter categories

VOICE 3.0 Online offers new filter categories for selecting speech events. In addition to domain and speech event type, you can choose events according to number of interactants/speakers, duration, number of words and several other metadata categories.

New style options

VOICE 3.0 Online offers an enhanced and highly customizable display of search results and full transcripts. Try out style options (voice, plain, pos, xml) and activate/deactivate kwic style for search results. In addition, you can adapt voice style according to your research needs by switching on/off different tag groups (overlaps, pauses, etc.; NEW: e.g. laughter, uncertain transcription).

Bookmarks and download

Save bookmarks and try out the new download function in VOICE 3.0 Online. Bookmarks for utterances can be locally stored at your own computer and can be ex-/imported. Search results and corpus texts can be downloaded in different file formats (.xlxs, .csv, .html, .txt). Downloaded files will mirror the style option chosen in the online interface.
